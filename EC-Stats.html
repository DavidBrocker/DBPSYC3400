Design \(S(A)\times B(C)\)

  • A and C are fixed

  • B and S are random

Random Factors are denoted by Greek Letters

  • \(\sigma^2\)

Fixed Factors are denoted by Roman Letters

  • \(\theta^2\)

Sources of variation:

Elementary Factors

-\(A\)

-\(B(C)\)

-\(C\)

-\(S(A)\)

Complex/Interaction Factors

-\(AC\)

-\(BS(AC)\)

-\(AB(C)\)

Obtain formulas for degrees of freedom

\(df_A = A-1\)

\(df_{B(C)} = C(B-1)\)

\(df_C = C-1\)

\(df_{S(A)} = A(S-1)\)

\(df_{AB(C)} =C(A-1)(B-1)\)

\(df_{AC} = (A-1)(C-1)\)

\(df_{BS(AC)} = AC(B-1)(S-1)\)
